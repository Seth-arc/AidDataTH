Overview

Our training employs a hybrid (blended virtual and in-person) model that combines foundational online courses delivered through William & Mary's Studio for Teaching & Learning Innovation with in-person workshops and policy dialogues. In-person sessions are country- and region-specific for targeted professional development. The curriculum blends academic research with practical methodologies, providing a comprehensive skillset for meaningful engagement with data and development finance.

About this Course

  • Learn the fundamentals of development finance in this comprehensive introductory course. You'll explore key concepts, funding sources, and implementation strategies that drive global development projects.

Who is this Course for?

  • Journalists covering international development, finance, and aid stories
  • Civil society organizations monitoring development projects and aid effectiveness
  • Advocacy groups focused on international development and aid

What You'll Learn

  • Master foundational statistical concepts and their application in development finance analysis
  • Transform complex data into compelling narratives through data journalism techniques
  • Develop proficiency in data analysis tools and visualization methods
  • Understand the evolution and dynamics of development finance mechanisms
  • Analyze contemporary finance strategies and their global implications
  • Evaluate the effectiveness of different finance delivery models

Course Structure

Module 1: Data Foundations

Begin your journey into development finance by mastering essential data analysis skills. Through hands-on practice with real datasets, you'll develop the analytical mindset needed to interpret complex metrics and transform raw information into actionable insights, setting the foundation for evidence-based decision-making.

Module 2: Data Journalism

Building on your analytical foundation, discover the power of data-driven storytelling in development finance. You'll learn to craft compelling narratives that illuminate complex financial data, while exploring how effective data journalism enhances transparency and drives meaningful change in the development sector.

Module 3: Critical Data Analysis and Visualization

Take your skills to the next level with advanced data analysis techniques. Working with real-world AidData scenarios, you'll master the art of extracting meaningful insights and creating impactful visualizations. This hands-on experience will empower you to make data-driven decisions that directly influence development strategies.

Module 4: Development Finance Models and Credit Provision

Cap your learning journey with a comprehensive exploration of development finance evolution. From post-WWII to today's dynamic landscape, you'll examine how traditional and emerging providers shape global development. Dive deep into China's rising influence and gain practical insights into evaluating various financing models' effectiveness.
