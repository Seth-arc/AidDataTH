Course Overview

Learn the fundamentals of development finance in this comprehensive introductory course. You'll explore key concepts, funding sources, and implementation strategies that drive global development projects.

What You'll Learn

  • Master foundational statistical concepts and their application in development finance analysis
  • Transform complex data into compelling narratives through data journalism techniques
  • Develop proficiency in data analysis tools and visualization methods
  • Understand the evolution and dynamics of development finance mechanisms
  • Analyze contemporary finance strategies and their global implications
  • Evaluate the effectiveness of different finance delivery models

Who This Course is For

  • Journalists covering international development, finance, and aid stories
  • Civil society organizations monitoring development projects and aid effectiveness
  • Media professionals interested in data-driven development journalism
  • NGO staff working on transparency and accountability in development finance
  • Advocacy groups focused on international development and aid
